		Sure you are.

			(whisper)

		Main Street.

								DISSOLVE TO:

	EXT. TOWN SQUARE. NIGHT.

	bonfire ...

	INT. SODA SHOP. LATER ...

				DAVID
		Maybe I have an idea.

								CUT TO:
	EXT. MAIN STREET. DAWN.

								CUT TO:

		I didn`t mean to hurt anybody.
